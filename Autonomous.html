Bharat Sevak Samaj (BSS)

Bharat Sevak Samaj (BSS) is a national development agency founded by the Government of India, under the Planning Commission, with the primary objective of promoting public cooperation in the implementation of various government plans and policies. Recognized by the Indian Parliament, BSS has been working to enhance education accessibility, social equality, and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all sections of society.

BSS is dedicated to improving public welfare by delivering education and skill development programs across India, particularly targeting marginalized and underserved communities. It has a strong focus on vocational education and training, oering a variety of courses through its established Vocational and IT Education Institutes. These institutes provide training in a wide range of elds, from technical skills to soft skills, aimed at preparing individuals for employment and self-sustainability.

The BSS National Vocational Training Mission is one of the major initiatives of Bharat Sevak Samaj, oering programs with durations ranging from 1 to 2 years. These programs are designed to equip students with practical skills that are highly valued in the job market, improving employability and economic stability. The courses are delivered through well-equipped training centers, which have modern infrastructure and in-house facilities that support hands-on learning and skill development.

In addition to its vocational training programs, BSS works toward the empowerment of individuals through its educational outreach, contributing to building a more skilled, inclusive, and socially equitable society. Through its varied initiatives, Bharat Sevak Samaj plays a vital role in supporting the nation’s socio-economic progress and human resource development.
